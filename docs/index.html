Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. The app runs 100% on your computer. Your WhatsApp messages, photos, and videos never leave your machine. We only process payments through Stripe — we never see your data.

What do I need?

A Mac (macOS 12+), an iPhone connected via USB, and a local iPhone backup (created via Finder). The app walks you through creating a backup if you don't have one.

Does it work with encrypted backups?

Currently, the app works with unencrypted backups. When creating your backup in Finder, make sure "Encrypt local backup" is unchecked.

Can I extract other apps?

Yes! With the Unlimited plan, you can extract data from any app in the backup — Camera Roll, IRCTC tickets, Apple Notes, Chrome, and more.

What formats are supported?

Chats are exported as readable .txt files. Media files (JPG, MP4, OPUS, PDF, etc.) are copied in their original format with correct dates.

Do I need to be online?

Only for the initial purchase/activation. After that, the Unlimited plan works completely offline.
